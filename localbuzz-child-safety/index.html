Child Safety Standards – Local Buzz

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Local Buzz is committed to maintaining a safe online environment and strictly prohibits any content or behavior that exploits, endangers, or harms children. We enforce strong policies and reporting mechanisms to prevent child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Local Buzz has a zero-tolerance policy for:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual exploitation of minors
  • Grooming or inappropriate interactions with minors
  • Any content depicting or promoting abuse of children

Any such content or behavior will result in immediate removal and permanent account suspension.

2. User Reporting System

Users can report harmful or suspicious content directly within the application. Reports may include:

  • Inappropriate posts
  • Suspicious user behavior
  • Content involving minors

3. Content Moderation

Local Buzz uses multiple safety measures including automated detection systems, manual moderation, and user reporting tools. Content that violates our policies will be removed immediately.

4. Cooperation With Authorities

Local Buzz complies with applicable child safety laws and will report confirmed cases to relevant law enforcement authorities.

5. Account Restrictions

Users violating child safety policies may face immediate account suspension, permanent ban, and reporting to authorities when required by law.

6. Safety Education

We encourage users to report suspicious behavior, avoid sharing personal information with strangers, and respect community guidelines.
